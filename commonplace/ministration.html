Ministration

Ministration (usually ministrations) noun: (1) formal, or humorous, the provision of assistance or care -- "the kitchen was made spotless by the ministrations of a cleaning lady." (2) the services of a minister of religion or of a religious institution -- "the ministrations of Father Martin"; the action of administering the sacrament -- "the celebration and ministration of the sacraments." Origin: late Middle English, from Latin ministratio(n-), from ministrare 'wait upon', from minister.
