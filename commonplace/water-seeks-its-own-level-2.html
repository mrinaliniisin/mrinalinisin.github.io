Water seeks its own level

The saying "water seeks its own level" is an expression that means people or things will naturally gravitate toward environments or situations that are similar to their own nature or level. It can also imply that people tend to associate with others who are similar to themselves in terms of values, beliefs, or social status. This saying is often used to illustrate the idea that individuals are drawn to environments where they feel comfortable and where they can thrive.
