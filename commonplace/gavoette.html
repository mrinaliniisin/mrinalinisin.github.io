Gavoette

Gavotte (noun): a medium-paced French dance, popular in the 18th century; also a piece of music accompanying or in the rhythm of a gavotte, composed in common time beginning on the third beat of the bar. Origin: French, from Provencal gavoto 'dance of the mountain people', from Gavot 'native of the Alps'.

Source: Oxford Dictionary (Apple Dictionary)
