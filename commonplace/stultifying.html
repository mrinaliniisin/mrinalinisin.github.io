Stultifying

Stultifying (adjective): tending to stifle enthusiasm, initiative, or freedom of action -- "the corporate environment, with its relentless emphasis on results, has a stultifying effect." Thesaurus (stultify, verb): "free market forces had been stultified by the welfare state" -- hamper, impede, obstruct, thwart, frustrate, foil. Example usage: "It can be stultifying to sit around."
